Why do i like Samurai Jack

Growing up Samurai jack was one of the cartoons that played on cartoon network who to those who knows was one of the greatest channels and it brought us samurai jack.
The cartoon is filled with so much action, comedy and also alot of intruige because just when you think jack is about to accomplish his goal something unforseen happens or just as it seems jack has nothing left he uses his old ways to pull himself out of a futuristic problem one of the only good shows that has both the past and furture and it gels really well in this case.
so i really like the character because there a lot of subtle lessons you can learn that you wont see as a kid watching it for the first time.
